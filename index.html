About Us

Water Source (Hebei) Water Purification Technology Co., Ltd. focuses on efficient and eco-friendly water purification solutions. We are committed to providing healthy and safe water treatment systems for households, businesses, and industries, protecting every drop of clean water.

Our Water Solutions

Home Water Purification

We offer various home products including reverse osmosis water purifiers and central softening systems to make drinking and household water safer and healthier.

Commercial Water Purification

Customized water purification systems for hotels, office buildings, schools, and other commercial settings, meeting high-flow and high-standard water requirements.

Industrial Water Treatment

Efficient removal of industrial wastewater pollutants and resource recovery to support sustainable green development.
